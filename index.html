About Us

4MC Engineering Services

4MC Engineering was established on January 2020 at Bancal, Carmona Cavite. Solely owned by Reynaldo Jr. Cristobal and humbly started with only 5 employees. Due to effort and hardwork, the business continued its progress and grown into a separate area to accommodate the needs / demands of the growing market.

On its new venture, 4MC is also in TRADING and Fabrication and Engineering Services to accommodate semi-conductor companies and as part of our business expansion. Our organization is supported by individuals who are fully equipped with knowledge and skills necessary in their line of work, topped with dedication in contributing for the achievement of our goals.

It is the 4MC ENGINEERING in Precision Tooling Company's policy to provide a safe, accident free and health environment. Safety must be one of the major consideration in all operation. People are our most important asset, and provide a safety environment is one of the Company's greatest responsibilities.
